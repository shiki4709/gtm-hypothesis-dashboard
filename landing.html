The agents

Each one handles a different part of your GTM workflow.

Lead Discovery

Hawki

Finds leads who engage with content in your space. Scrapes LinkedIn post engagers, filters by ICP, drafts personalized messages.

  • Scrape 1,000+ engagers per post
  • ICP filtering with custom keywords
  • AI-drafted personalized messages
  • Message tracking + pipeline
  • Influencer watch list
Engagement Monitor

Pingi

Never miss a reply. Monitors Gmail, X, and LinkedIn for conversations that need your response. AI-drafted replies in seconds.

  • Multi-platform inbox monitoring
  • Priority-scored notifications
  • AI-drafted context-aware replies
  • Follow-up tracking
Content Creation

Roari

Turn any source into platform-native posts. Create content for LinkedIn, X, Substack, and more. Build your brand on autopilot.

  • Multi-platform publishing
  • Source-to-post conversion
  • Brand voice matching
  • Content calendar
